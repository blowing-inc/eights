PRIORITY 1 — Guest Nudges (Player Continuity First)
================================================================================

CLAUDE.md says: nudge guests toward login at natural moments (joining,
draft submit, etc.). Don't lecture — just make the benefit clear.
Currently: zero guest nudge moments exist in the app.

Three moments to add, in order of value:

--- 1A. CreateRoom.jsx ---
Moment: after the guest fills in their name and is about to create a room.
What to add: one-line nudge beneath the name field (or near the submit button)
  e.g. "Playing as guest? Log in to save your history across devices."
  Only show if user is not logged in (check currentUser prop).
  Should link to / trigger the login flow, not block creation.

--- 1B. DraftScreen.jsx ---
Moment: after the guest taps the submit/lock confirmation.
What to add: a brief one-liner on the post-submit state
  e.g. "Lock in your picks? Log in so you can always rejoin this game."
  Appears in the confirmation step, not as a wall before submit.
  Same condition: only for guests.

--- 1C. VoteScreen.jsx ---
Moment: when a guest casts their first vote (round 1 or first time voting).
What to add: a dismissible banner or inline note after the vote is recorded.
  e.g. "Nice pick. Log in so you never lose your spot if you switch devices."
  One-time show (use local state, don't re-show each vote).
  Same condition: only for guests.

Implementation note: all three should use the same visual treatment for
consistency — check how existing nudge UI is styled (if any) or pick one
pattern and apply it to all three. Keep it subtle: inline text with a
login link, not a modal.
