[DONE] PRIORITY 1 — Guest Nudges (Player Continuity First)
================================================================================

CLAUDE.md says: nudge guests toward login at natural moments (joining,
draft submit, etc.). Don't lecture — just make the benefit clear.

All three nudge moments implemented. Consistent visual treatment: inline
text at warning/tertiary color with an underlined "Log in →" link button.
No modals, no walls.

--- 1A. CreateRoom.jsx --- [DONE]
Implemented stronger than spec: instead of a generic "save your history"
nudge, guests creating a room see a host-specific warning — "You're creating
as a guest. If you close this tab or switch devices, you'll lose host controls
and the game can't continue. Log in first →"
Only shown when isGuest is true. Calls goAuth('create') to return after auth.
A second confirmation gate was also added at lobby start: if the host is still
a guest when they tap "Start game", a one-tap prompt appears with "Log in first"
or "Start anyway" before the game begins.

--- 1B. DraftScreen.jsx --- [DONE]
After submitting the draft, guests see below the "locked in" confirmation:
"Playing as guest — if you switch devices or refresh, you might lose your spot.
Log in to stay connected →"
Calls goAuth('draft') so auth returns to the draft-submitted state.

--- 1C. VoteScreen.jsx --- [DONE]
After a guest casts their first pick, a one-time dismissible note appears:
"Playing as guest — switching devices could lose your spot. Log in → ✕"
Tapping "Log in →" navigates to auth AND dismisses; ✕ dismisses without
navigating. Local state (voteNudgeDone) ensures it shows only once per
session. Calls goAuth('vote') to return to the vote screen after auth.

Auth return flow: all five onLogin entry points (create, join, lobby, draft,
vote) now call goAuth(targetScreen). goAuth is the single entry point — sets
afterAuth and navigates to auth. UserPill continues to call goAuth() without
an argument, falling back to the current screen.

Guest continuity improvements shipped alongside this work (see
docs/guest-handling-design.txt for full spec):
  - guestId moved from sessionStorage to localStorage (survives refresh/tab close)
  - ?pid= URL param in rejoin links reclaims guest identity across devices
  - ConnectionStatus component: always-visible presence dot strip on all in-room
    screens; host can tap a disconnected player to copy a rejoin link
  - trackRoomPresence extended to expose all present player IDs
  - JoinRoom shows "N open games →" link for guests with active lobbies
  - Admin UsersTab: new "Re-attribute guest history" section to link old guest
    combatants/rooms to a newly-registered account
